Patient-Therapist

Emotion detection technology allows for personalized therapeutic interventions. By recognizing patterns in the patient’s emotional responses over time, therapists can tailor treatment plans based on what triggers certain emotional reactions. This personalization can improve therapeutic outcomes by addressing underlying issues more effectively.

By analyzing a patient’s facial expressions throughout therapy sessions, therapists can receive instant feedback on how the patient is responding to different topics or therapeutic methods. For patients undergoing long-term therapy, emotion detection can assist in monitoring progress by tracking emotional responses over multiple sessions. Conversely, an increase in negative emotional states may signal the need to adjust the therapeutic approach.
