Security and Surveillance

Security and Surveillance refer to the comprehensive systems and technologies designed to monitor, detect, and respond to threats or suspicious activities in various environments. These systems play a critical role in safeguarding physical locations, assets, and individuals by utilizing a combination of hardware and software solutions.

Continuous camera observation enables security personnel or automated systems to detect unusual behavior or threats in real-time. Video surveillance, such as CCTV, is commonly used for live monitoring. Advanced systems can now analyze facial expressions to detect emotions like stress, fear, or aggression, offering deeper insights into human behavior and improving security by predicting potential risks.
